PROJECT GOAL:

A read-only National Guard resource app for soldiers.



NO CLASSIFIED INFORMATION

NO OPERATIONAL DATA

NO MEDICAL DATA

NO EVALUATIONS



CORE FEATURES:

\- Unit-based resource hub

\- Command memos (PDFs or links)

\- Points of contact per unit

\- Role-based access (soldier, admin)



OUT OF SCOPE:

\- Messaging or chat

\- Soldier tracking

\- Discipline or counseling

\- Sensitive PII
